# Lexuz Tours & Adventures Source Facts

**Confirmed facts:** founded **2018** · founder's personal passion for travelling the northern areas · first trip marketed with **hand-printed flyers/posters distributed manually to people and students nearby** (3–4 posters) · first trip drew **110 participants** · first destination: **Miranjani Top** (Galiyat) · weekly departures to different destinations ever since.
## 1. Company Story

> Lexuz Tours started with a few printed posters and a personal obsession.
>
> In 2018, our founder Hassnaat Khan wasn't running a company — he was just a traveller who couldn't stay away from the mountains. One day he decided to plan a trip north, printed three or four posters by hand, and distributed flyers to people and students around the neighbourhood: *I'm going to Miranjani Top. Come with me.*
>
> One hundred and ten people showed up.
>
> There was no online marketing behind it, no booking system, no office — just a trip organised by someone who genuinely loved the road. That first climb to Miranjani Top became the first Lexuz departure, and it set the pattern the company still follows: trips run by people who travel because they love it, not because it's a product.
>
> From that day, Lexuz Tours & Adventures has run departures every week — to Hunza, Skardu, Naran, Swat, Kumrat, Neelum and beyond — growing from a hand-distributed flyer into a registered company (SECP and FBR) with its own branded fleet and a team that has spent years on these roads.
>
> The posters got better. The reason for the trips never changed.
