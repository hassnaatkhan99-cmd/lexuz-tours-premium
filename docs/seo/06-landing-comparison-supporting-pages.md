## PART 5 — COMPARISON PAGES (structure + verdict logic)

Structure for all: intro (name the dilemma in sentence 1) → side-by-side comparison table → 6–8 factor sections (scenery, travel time from ISB/LHR, road comfort, family friendliness, budget factors, best season, crowd levels, food & stay) → "choose X if / choose Y if" verdict → FAQs (8–10) → CTAs to both tours. Schema: Article + ItemList + FAQPage.

1. **Hunza vs Skardu** (/compare/hunza-vs-skardu/) — Verdict logic: Hunza for first-timers, couples, easier roads, culture and comfort; Skardu for lakes, Deosai, cold desert, and travellers who don't mind rougher approach roads or want the flight option. Links: both tour pages (ISB+LHR), both pillars.
2. **Swat vs Kumrat** (/compare/swat-vs-kumrat/) — Swat for comfort, hotels, family ease, winter access (Malam Jabba); Kumrat for raw forest, camping, jeep adventure (jeep charges note). 
3. **Fairy Meadows vs Naran** (/compare/fairy-meadows-vs-naran/) — FM for the trek, Nanga Parbat views, adventurous groups (jeep track + 2–3 hr hike, jeep charges note); Naran for drive-up access, families, Saif-ul-Malook.
4. **Neelum Valley vs Hunza** (/compare/neelum-valley-vs-hunza/) — Neelum for lush green, rivers, shorter drive, budget-friendlier; Hunza for high Karakoram drama, forts, blossom/autumn seasons.
5. **Skardu by Road vs by Air** (/compare/skardu-by-road-vs-by-air/) — Flight saves a day each way but is weather-cancellable; road is reliable and scenic but long. Honest: tours run by road for group reliability.
6. **Summer vs Winter Pakistan Tours** (/compare/summer-vs-winter-pakistan-tours/) — season table by destination; what closes in winter (Babusar, Deosai, high jeep tracks) vs what shines (snow Murree/Malam Jabba, quiet winter Hunza).
7. **Private vs Group Tours** (/compare/private-vs-group-tours/) — cost efficiency and social side of groups vs flexibility and pace of private; honest fit-finder, links to How to Book.
